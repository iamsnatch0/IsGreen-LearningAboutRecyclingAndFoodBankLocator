Network of 11 food banks sharing Hyndburn £127k funding pot

A network of food banks across Hyndburn is being co-ordinated to share emergency government funding of more than £120,000 to help those in urgent need of help. A £127,658 Emergency Assistance Grant (EAG) was allocated to the borough to help people in immediate short-term need of resources, such as food or essential items. The government grant will be delivered in by charity Maundy Relief, of Accrington, Lancashire through a network of 11 local food banks.
